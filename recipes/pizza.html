Pizza Recipe

It's time for homemade pepperoni pizza

Fancy the authentic Italian pizza without going to Italy? Then how about a homemade Italian recipe.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Mozzarella
  • Dough
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Prep the dough
  2. Crush the tomatoes
  3. Put the tomato sauce on the dough
  4. Sprinkle the mozzarella on the pizza
  5. Pop in the oven for 20 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
