Salad Recipe

Refreshing salad for all occassions

Fancy a nice, refreshing salad. Look no further than our amazing salad, great for any occassion.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Olives
  • Feta

Steps

  1. Wash the lettuce
  2. Cut the tomatoes
  3. Mix everything together
  4. Add a splash of olive oil
  5. Serve!
